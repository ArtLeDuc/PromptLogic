# Code of Conduct
## PromptLogic Community Guidelines
PromptLogic is built around clarity, modularity, and teachability — and the community around it should reflect those same values.<br>This Code of Conduct outlines the expectations for everyone interacting with the project, whether in Issues, Discussions, pull requests, or the Feedback Form.
The goal is simple:
  Create a space where people can collaborate, ask questions, and share ideas without friction or hostility.

## Our Pledge
We commit to making participation in the PromptLogic community a respectful, harassment‑free experience for everyone, regardless of:
- Background
- Experience level
- Identity
- Technical skill
- Personal style
- Communication ability
We want contributors, testers, educators, and creators to feel welcome and supported.
---
## Our Standards
Examples of behavior that support a healthy community:
- Being respectful and constructive
- Offering thoughtful feedback
- Asking questions with curiosity, not judgment
- Acknowledging different perspectives
- Keeping discussions focused on the work
- Helping others understand the architecture and philosophy of the project<br>Examples of unacceptable behavior:
  - Personal attacks or insults
  - Harassment or discriminatory language
  - Dismissing or belittling others’ questions
  - Aggressive or hostile communication
  - Posting content unrelated to the project
  - Pushing changes or ideas without discussion
  - Ignoring the project’s architectural principles<br>
  PromptLogic is a space for clarity and collaboration — not conflict.
---
## Responsibilities of Maintainers
Maintainers (currently the project owner) are responsible for:
- Reviewing Issues, Discussions, and pull requests
- Setting the architectural direction of the project
- Clarifying expectations and design principles
- Moderating behavior that violates this Code of Conduct
- Removing or rejecting contributions that introduce hostility or chaos
- Guiding contributors toward productive collaboration<br>Maintainers have the right to take action when needed to keep the community healthy.
---
## Enforcement
If someone engages in unacceptable behavior, maintainers may take actions such as:
- A gentle reminder about the guidelines
- Editing or removing inappropriate comments
- Closing or locking Issues or Discussions
- Rejecting pull requests
- Temporarily or permanently restricting participation<br>These actions are taken at the maintainers’ discretion to protect the project and its community.
---
## Reporting Issues
If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct:
- Open a private Issue (if appropriate)
- Use the Feedback Form
- Or contact the maintainer directly
Reports will be handled respectfully and confidentially.
---
## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies to:
- The GitHub repository
- Issues and Discussions
- Pull requests
- Community interactions
- Any official PromptLogic communication channels
It applies equally to maintainers, contributors, and users.
---
## Final Thoughts
PromptLogic is a tool built for creators, educators, and performers — people who rely on clarity, trust, and predictability.<br>The community should reflect those same values.
<br>Thank you for helping make this a welcoming and productive space.
